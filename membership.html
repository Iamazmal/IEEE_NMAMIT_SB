Join IEEE NMAMIT Student Branch

  1. Fill out the online membership application form
  2. Select your membership type: Student, Graduate, or Professional
  3. Choose your preferred chapters and affinity groups
  4. After adding and selecting chapters, click on the "Checkout" button
  5. Women in Engineering (WIE) Membership: Female students can join IEEE Women in Engineering at no additional cost! Simply select the WIE membership option during checkout. This gives you access to exclusive WIE resources, events, and networking opportunities.
  6. Complete the payment process using credit/debit card
  7. Receive a confirmation email with your membership details
  8. Join us at our next event to meet fellow members!
